Our Story

Basreng Ngereyeuh was born from a passion for authentic Indonesian flavors and a desire to share our cultural heritage through food. What started as a small home kitchen experiment in 2018 has grown into a beloved local brand.

Our founder, Michael Williams, discovered the unique taste of basreng during his travels through Indonesia and was inspired to create his own version with a focus on quality ingredients and authentic flavors.

Our Values

  • Quality ingredients sourced from local suppliers

  • Traditional recipes with modern innovation

  • Supporting local communities through employment

  • Sustainable packaging and responsible practices
